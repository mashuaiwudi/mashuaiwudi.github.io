Welcome to participate in this experiment. The whole experiment consists of 4 sessions and each takes around 3min to finish. In each session, you are required to answer ALL questions.

Robots learn to play sports

Now assuming you have a challenging task to teach four robots four kinds of skills, hand-shaking, boxing, playing the piano, playing the drum. Suppose you recruit professional coaches to help teach and train robots remotely. Your task is to rate your team members' performances during the learning process, and evaluate whether they are good learners.

For each team member (the robot), it needs to learn one sports skill in two steps. First of all, a professional coach will demonstrate offline how to do that sports skill ( Check video below: Step 1 Demonstration ). Second, after the demonstration, the robot will be trained by an coach avatar in an online environment ( Check video below: Step 2 Learning ). After the second step, you need to rate the robot's learning performance as well as its learning ability.

One possible question to be asked:

  • The robot was engaged in learning the skill.
