About Me

Shuai is a Master degree candidate in the Beijing Key Lab of Human-Computer Interaction at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. His primary research focuses on using both more traditional Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) approaches and cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques to improve the interaction between humans and machines. His research interests include Intelligent User Interfaces, Mobile Interfaces, Interactive Machine Learning, and their applications in Life, Education and Healthcare. Shuai received his Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology.
