Welcome to participate in this experiment! In this experiment, we will show you 20 short news in sequence, and your task is to classify the topic of each news. The correct answer will be one out of the five categories, 1. Business, 2. Entertainment, 3. Healthy Living, 4. Politics, and 5. Technology.

Task Procedure

You need to first (1) make an initial judgment independently, then (2) we will provide you with the help of AI, and (3) finally you need to make the final judgment.

The AI is trained on a huge news data set. We previously evaluated this AI on a large data set and its accuracy was 90% (i.e., the AI's predictions were correct on 90% of the news in that data set.)


The whole process will take you about 10-20 minutes. And we will pay a 1 USD bonus if your prediction accuracy >= 95% in the 20 tasks.

We will conduct an attention check. If you fail the attention check, we may not pay you.
