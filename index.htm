Shuai Ma is a Master Degree candidate at Human-Computer Interaction Lab, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS), supervised by Prof. Feng Tian.

His primary research focuses on AI-supported HCI and Human-engaged AI. His research interests include User Modeling, AI-Human Collaboration, Interactive Machine Learning and their applications in Daily tasks, Healthcare, Multi-media interaction, Education in MOOCs, etc. He received his Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT).

Shuai will graduate in 2020 Summer and he is applying for a Ph.D. offer in HCI.
