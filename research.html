SmartEye: Assisting Instant Photo Taking via Integrating User Preference with Deep View Proposal Network

with Zijun Wei at ISCAS and Adobe

Instant photo taking and sharing has become one of the most popular forms of social networking. However, taking high-quality photos is a difficult task as it requires expertise in composition. In this paper, we present a novel mobile system to help users make good photo compositions in-situ. The back-end of our system is view proposal network (VPN), a deep learning model that processes images and generates composition suggestions in real time. To enable personalization and user adaptation, we extend the model by integrating user preference modeling, which learns to adjust the VPN based on user feedback. We also present novel interfaces to make the system easy to use. User study results show that our proposed model can learn and adapt to users’ preferences, and outperformed pure VPN in suggesting photo compositions.
